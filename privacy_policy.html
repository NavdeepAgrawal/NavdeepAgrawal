Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 16-October-2025

What Tara Frame Does

Tara Frame is a creative platform that allows users to upload personal images and place orders for fridge magnets and lab photo printouts.

We respect your privacy and handle your data carefully, strictly for processing and delivering your orders.

Information We Collect

  • Uploaded Images: Only the images you upload to create your products.
  • Order Information: Product preferences, quantity, etc.
  • Delivery Information: Name, address, and contact number.
  • Device Info: Basic information for improving app performance.

How We Use Your Data

  • To create and fulfill your orders.
  • To provide customer support.
  • To improve app functionality and reliability.

We never use your data for advertising or sell it to third parties.

Image Handling & Storage

Uploaded images are stored securely and temporarily. All images are deleted after the order is fulfilled.

We use encrypted connections and secure servers to protect your content.

Third-Party Services

Tara Frame may use trusted third-party services for:

  • Payment processing
  • Order delivery
  • App analytics (e.g., Firebase)

All third-party providers are privacy-compliant (e.g., GDPR, CCPA).

What We Don’t Do

  • ❌ We do not sell or share your personal data.
  • ❌ We do not use your images for anything other than printing.
  • ❌ We do not track your activity across other apps or websites.
